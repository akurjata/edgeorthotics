About

Hello! I am a multimedia journalist covering Northern British Columbia for CBC Radio and cbc.ca.

I also write a culture column for Thimbleberry Magazine, and in my spare time I try to run the r/princegeorge community subreddit.

My pronouns are he and him, and I helped produce a podcast about why this matters.

I am based in the city of Prince George, on the traditional and unceded territory of the Lheidli T'enneh First Nation. Here's a city user's guide I wrote.

In case you're wondering, my last name is pronounced "ker • ya • ta".
